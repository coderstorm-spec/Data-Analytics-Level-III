## Project Overview

Bahaghari Retail is a growing retail company that operates stores across the three major 
regions of the Philippines—Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. To support data-driven 
decision-making, the company conducted an analysis of its sales transactions using 
Power BI. The project aimed to examine sales performance by region, product category, 
and individual products to identify trends, customer purchasing behavior, and revenue drivers. 
By transforming and visualizing transaction data, the analysis provided valuable insights 
into which products contribute most to sales, how sales vary across regions, and what 
opportunities exist to improve inventory management, marketing strategies, 
and overall business performance.
